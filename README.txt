For Player movement  controls please use arrow keys;
To choose difficulty, it will be done via the Q,A,Z key.
difficulty modifies the rate which mobs move at.
You can pick up Weapons with the spacebar.
when u have picked up the axe/sword there will be a indicator on the left,
to use the axe press the spacebar
to use the sword press B
the weapons are only usable once a movement 
the sword will strike in the last direction u moved in for 2 blocks infront
the axe will strike around you
to win, navigate yourself to the left most opening in the centre of the maze.
