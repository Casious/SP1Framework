How to play our game : Navigate your way out of the maze and try not to die to mobs that spawn.
Controls:
Directional arrow keys to move one space (E.g. ↑, ↓,→,←)
Spacebar to pick up weapon (If player is on the same space as the weapon itself)
Attack Controls:
Spacebar to attack with the cross (Expels a white aura around the player that kills in a radius of one tile around the player)
B to attack with the sword (Attacks a column of 3 tiles in the direction the player previously moved, e.g. If the player moves up, the sword attacks 3 tiles above the player, if a wall is in front of the player, sword range is restricted from player to wall.)
